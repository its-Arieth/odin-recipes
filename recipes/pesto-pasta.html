Pesto Pasta

What is Pesto Pasta?

A proof that blending a garden into a sauce can somehow taste like heaven. It's the only dish where you proudly eat leaves and call it gourmet!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 Â½ tablespoons pesto or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • sea salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Step By Step

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  2. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
